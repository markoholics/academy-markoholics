The Problem

Most marketers are using AI like a fancy Google search.

That's why they're not getting results — and why companies are still looking for someone who actually knows how to wield it.

03

The Fix

Here's what senior marketers actually do.

They use AI as a system — not a shortcut. They have a prompt library. A content engine. A way to turn data into narrative without being a data analyst. They know what to automate and what to keep human.

This course teaches exactly that — in a live cohort setting, with a real instructor in the room who has spent 6+ years at Monash Business School, UpGrad, and LearnTube watching where the gaps are.

Each session ends with something you can use, show, or deploy immediately. You learn with a cohort — not alone, not asynchronously.

Foundation Mental Models AI Stack
