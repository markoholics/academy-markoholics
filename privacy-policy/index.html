Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026  ·  Effective date: 17 June 2026

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to H2AI Technologies ("H²AI Academy", "we", "us", or "our"), operating the website academy.humantothepowerofai.com (the "Academy").

Registered address: 530/3, Kopali Road, Zakirnagar, Mango, Jamshedpur, Jharkhand 832110, India.

This policy is published in compliance with the Information Technology Act, 2000 (including the IT (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Data or Information) Rules, 2011) and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDP Act").

2. Personal Data We Collect

We collect personal data when you voluntarily provide it during enrolment, enquiry, or use of the Academy website. Categories include:

  • Identity & Contact Data: Full name, email address, phone number, and WhatsApp number.
  • Enrolment & Payment Data: Tier selection, payment reference, and transaction confirmation. Full payment processing is handled by our third-party payment gateway; we do not store card or banking credentials.
  • Academic Data: Cohort attendance, session participation, assignment submissions, and course progress where applicable.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, device type, pages visited, time spent, referring URL, and IP address, collected automatically via analytics tools.
  • Cookie Data: Small text files placed on your device (see Section 8).

We do not collect sensitive personal data (as defined under the DPDP Act) such as government identification numbers, health records, or biometric data.

3. Purpose and Use of Your Data

  • Processing your enrolment and managing your access to live sessions, recordings, and course materials.
  • Communicating course schedules, updates, session links, and support via email and WhatsApp.
  • Processing refund requests in accordance with our refund policy.
  • Sending you course-related and (where consented) marketing communications about future programmes.
  • Improving the Academy's website, content, and learning experience through analytics.
  • Complying with applicable Indian law and legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the DPDP Act, 2023, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Consent: Where you have given free, specific, informed, and unambiguous consent — for example, by completing enrolment or subscribing to communications.
  • Contractual Necessity: Where processing is necessary to fulfil our obligations to you under the enrolment agreement.
  • Legitimate Use: Where processing is necessary for legitimate business purposes that do not override your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Where required to comply with applicable laws or legal processes.

5. Sharing and Disclosure of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share it in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Supabase for enrolment data; payment gateway providers; video conferencing platforms for live sessions.
  • Analytics & Marketing: Aggregated data shared with Meta Pixel and analytics platforms.
  • Legal Requirements: Where required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, with prior notice to you.

6. Data Retention

  • Enrolment and contact data: retained for up to 3 years from last interaction, unless you request earlier deletion.
  • Payment records: retained for 7 years as required under Indian accounting and tax law.
  • Analytics and usage data: retained for up to 26 months in aggregate form.

7. Your Rights Under the DPDP Act 2023

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a summary of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data where no longer required.
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: Lodge a complaint with our Grievance Officer. If unresolved, approach the Data Protection Board of India.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to Nominate: Nominate another individual to exercise these rights on your behalf.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand site usage in aggregate, anonymised form.
  • Marketing Pixels: We use the Meta Pixel (Facebook) to measure advertising effectiveness. This involves transfer of data to Meta Platforms, Inc. (USA). By using our website, you consent to this use.

9. Security

We implement reasonable security practices as mandated under the IT (Reasonable Security Practices) Rules, 2011, including HTTPS encryption, access controls, and secure cloud infrastructure via Supabase.

10. Children's Privacy

Our courses are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If a child has provided us with data, please contact our Grievance Officer and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Academy following any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
